# Sora Features

## Overview

Sora is an AI-powered study companion that helps students learn more effectively by combining proven learning techniques with artificial intelligence.

Instead of simply tracking study time, Sora actively helps students remember, understand, and apply what they learn.

---

# Core Features

## 1. Smart Study Planner

* Add subjects and topics.
* Set study goals.
* Organize learning sessions.
* Create personalized study schedules.

---

## 2. AI Learning Assistant

After a study session, Sora can:

* Summarize key concepts.
* Identify the most important topics using the 80/20 principle.
* Highlight common mistakes.
* Recommend what to study next.

---

## 3. Active Recall

Automatically generate:

* Short-answer questions
* Multiple-choice questions
* True/False questions
* Coding questions (for programming)
* Numerical problems (for mathematics and statistics)

---

## 4. Flashcard Generator

Create personalized flashcards from study notes and learning materials.

---

## 5. Spaced Repetition

Automatically schedule review sessions based on previous performance.

Students never need to manually decide when to revise.

---

## 6. Mistake Tracker

Sora remembers:

* Incorrect answers
* Weak concepts
* Frequently forgotten topics

These concepts appear more often in future quizzes until they are mastered.

---

## 7. Mandatory Practice

Every topic ends with an application task.

Examples:

* Coding exercise
* Mini project
* Problem-solving assignment
* Written report

Learning is only considered complete after applying the concept.

---

## 8. Progress Dashboard

Track:

* Study streak
* Topics completed
* Recall accuracy
* Quiz performance
* Flashcard progress
* Weak areas
* Strong areas

---

## 9. Surprise Quiz

Sora occasionally generates surprise quizzes from previously studied topics to reinforce long-term memory.

---

## 10. Interview Mode

Generate interview-style questions after mastering a topic.

Supports:

* Programming
* Data Science
* Statistics
* Mathematics
* Technical concepts
