## 📖 About Sora

Sora is an AI-powered study companion designed to help students learn more effectively—not by studying longer, but by studying smarter.

Unlike traditional study apps that only store notes or track study time, Sora actively participates in the learning process. It helps students remember concepts through active recall, reinforces knowledge using spaced repetition, identifies weak areas, and encourages practical application through projects and tasks.

Sora is designed to feel like a trusted study partner that guides, challenges, and supports students throughout their learning journey.

---

# ❗ The Problem

Many students spend hours studying but quickly forget what they have learned. They often reread notes, highlight textbooks, or watch videos repeatedly, but these passive methods do not lead to long-term understanding.

Existing study apps mainly focus on note-taking, reminders, or flashcards. Very few combine proven learning techniques into one personalized learning experience.

Students need more than a planner.

They need a study partner.

---

# 💡 Our Solution

Sora transforms every study session into an active learning experience.

After each session, Sora automatically:

* 🧠 Generates Active Recall questions
* 🃏 Creates personalized flashcards
* 🎲 Creates surprise quizzes
* 📅 Schedules future reviews using spaced repetition
* 🎯 Highlights the most important concepts using the 80/20 principle
* 📊 Tracks weak topics and common mistakes
* 💻 Assigns practical tasks or mini-projects to apply new knowledge
* 📈 Adapts future quizzes based on the student's progress

Instead of simply asking *"Did you study?"*, Sora asks:

> **"Can you prove you learned it?"**
